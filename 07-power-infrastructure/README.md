# Power Infrastructure

This document describes the electrical setup used to power the workstation and homelab systems.

---

## Current Limitation

Only one wall outlet is available near the workstation.

This previously resulted in multiple daisy-chained power strips.

This configuration was considered unsafe due to potential overload risk.

---

## Planned Upgrade

A new surge-protected power strip will replace the previous configuration.

Requirements:

- 8 sockets
- Surge protection
- Grounded EU (Schuko) sockets
- Long cable (2–3 meters)

---

## Purpose

The upgraded power strip will:

Provide safe power distribution  
Reduce cable clutter  
Protect hardware from voltage spikes

---

Gaming PC

Peripherals
